## Explain about core concepts
- In this game, your hero will try to prevent the bugs from hitting himself/herself/itself by shooting at the bugs
- The bugs will be coming from 5 directions towards you
- You can use these 5 buttons to fire drug (antibiotics) at the bugs to destroy them
- [Show them the directions and how it's corresponding to the directions of the bug]
- You can use the 3 buttons at the back to choose the type of drug (left, right, middle)
- [Show them the buttons and how it's corresponding to the type of drugs on screen]
- A bug will be destroyed if it's not resistant to a drug
- If a bug doesn't have any colors, it means it doesn't have any resistance, you can use any drugs
- But if a bug have red color on the head, it means the bug is resisting to the red drug already, you will need to use a drug of different colors (green/blue)
- A bug can have up to 2 kind of drug resistance, and your only choice is to use the remaining color

## Explain about resistance bar
- The ideal scenario is the participant panic and spam the button/only stick with a drug, this is a good chance to talk about the resistance bar

- The more frequent you use a type of drug, the higher the bar for that drug will be
- Similarly, if you don't use a drug for a long period, the bar for that drug will drop lower
- The higher a bar for a type of drug, the more "drug resistant" bugs will appear

- So a good strategy is to have less drug resistant bugs = keep these bars under control = not to spam antibiotics everywhere. It means that you use drug precisely, and switch up between the drugs
